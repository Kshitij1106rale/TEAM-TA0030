# **App Name**: AgriVision AI Platform

## Core Features:

- AI-Powered Disease Detection: Enables farmers to upload leaf images for AI-driven disease prediction and instant diagnostics, offering a tool to quickly identify and address potential crop health issues.
- Real-time Mandi Price Comparison: Aggregates and compares real-time mandi prices to identify the highest-paying markets for their produce, ensuring optimal selling decisions and increased profitability.
- Profit Estimation Engine: Utilizes machine learning to estimate profit based on market trends, weather data, and crop health, enabling pre-harvest financial planning and informed decision-making.
- Treatment & Prevention Recommendations: Offers customized treatment and preventative strategies post-disease detection, assisting farmers in mitigating risks and sustaining crop health through expert recommendations.
- Weather-Based Farming Advisory: Provides farming advisory based on real-time weather updates and forecasts, which assists in reducing environmental risks and helps adjust farming practices for better yields.
- Secure User Dashboard: Offers a secure dashboard to manage all farming data, monitor crop health, view market prices, and access AI-driven recommendations, promoting enhanced user engagement and accessibility.
- Multilingual Support: Supports multiple regional languages to ensure the platform is accessible and usable for all small and marginal farmers, transcending language barriers.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Forest green (#386641) evoking nature and growth.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC), providing a neutral and accessible feel.
- Accent color: Earthy orange (#A68A64) to highlight essential information and CTAs.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif with a balance of modern look and warmth; suitable for headlines or body text.
- Use flat, minimalist icons that visually represent farming, weather, and economic elements; aim for clarity.
- Clean, user-friendly layout that organizes data in an accessible manner. Dashboard should be easily navigable with intuitive sections for each core feature.
- Subtle transitions for data loading and updates, enhancing user experience without being distracting.
